My Location

Frederick, Maryland, is a vibrant city located about an hour’s drive from both Washington, D.C., and Baltimore. Known for its perfectly preserved 18th and 19th century architecture, the area serves as a scenic gateway between the bustling metropolitan centers to the south and the rolling hills of the Catoctin Mountains to the north. It is the second-largest city in the state, offering a unique blend of colonial history and modern biotech industries.
